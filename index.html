What is Broken Shadows?

Broken Shadows is a puzzle platformer centered around the idea of visibility and manipulating (you guessed it) light. In its later stages, the game will feature non-linear exploration, hazards to avoid, enemies to defeat, and various ways with which to beat an area. The game comes with a built-in map editor to create custom levels.
